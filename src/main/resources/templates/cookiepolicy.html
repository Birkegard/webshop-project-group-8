What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you use our service. They help the service function properly and improve your experience.

Types of cookies we use

  • Functional cookies – remember your settings
  • Analytics cookies – help us understand how the service is used

Third-party cookies

We may use third-party services (such as analytics tools) that place cookies on your device.

Your choices

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.
